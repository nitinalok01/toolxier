How This Works

This tool helps you easily resize any image to fit your needs—whether it’s for social media, printing, website optimization, or document attachment.

  • Upload: Choose any image file (max 5MB) using drag & drop or the browse button.
  • Select Size: Use standard dimensions or enter your own custom width and height.
  • Resize: Click "Resize" to generate your new image instantly.
  • Download: You can then download the resized image as JPG, PNG, or even a compact PDF.

Why resize? Resizing helps reduce image file size, improve load speeds on websites, and fit dimensions for apps, emails, or online forms.

This all happens locally in your browser—your images never leave your device. It's safe, private, and fast.
