About This GST Calculator

This GST calculator helps you quickly calculate GST amount and total price based on the selected tax rate. Whether you're a seller or a buyer, it’s useful for understanding how much tax is applied to a product or service.

  • Step 1: Enter the base amount in ₹.
  • Step 2: Select a standard GST rate (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) or choose custom.
  • Step 3: Choose the type of GST calculation:
    • Exclusive: GST will be added to the base amount.
    • Inclusive: GST is already included in the amount.
  • Step 4: Click Calculate to get Net, GST, and Total.

This tool runs completely in your browser and helps with fast & accurate tax calculations.
